Our Temple

Sri Vijaya Sunandaramaya is a buddhist temple and a meditation center located in Panangalawatta, Poruwadanda, Sri Lanka. The chief incumbent of the temple Sri Bodhirajaramaya, Rambakulama, Medawachchiya, in Sri Lanka the most venerable Poonawe Sumanajothi Nayaka Thero commenced the active development of the temple Sri Vijaya Sunandaramaya, Panangala, Poruwadanda in Sri Lanka on the first of January in the year 2006. Our temple is open for both local and foreign devotees to conduct meditation activities and steps have been taken to give the social services on the field of Indigenous Ayurvedic Treatments and Astrology.
